## Features

-   **Select:** Click and drag to select multiple cells.
-   **Select All:** Use <kbd>Ctrl/Cmd+A</kbd> to select all cells in the grid.
-   **Edit:** Double-click a cell or start typing on a selected cell. Press Enter to save, Escape to cancel.
-   **Column Settings:** Right-click a column header to rename it or change its data type.
-   **Sort & Filter:** Hover over a column header to reveal sort and filter icons.
-   **Copy/Paste:** Use <kbd>Ctrl/Cmd+C</kbd> and <kbd>Ctrl/Cmd+V</kbd>. Works with Excel and Google Sheets!
-   **Drag-Fill:** Click and drag the small square on the bottom-right of a selection to copy values.
-   **Fill Down:** Double-click the fill handle to copy the selection down until the next non-empty cell.
-   **Navigate:** Use arrow keys to move the active cell.
-   **Find & Replace:** Press <kbd>Ctrl/Cmd+F</kbd> or <kbd>Ctrl/Cmd+R</kbd> to open the find and replace panel.
-   **Undo/Redo:** Use <kbd>Ctrl/Cmd+Z</kbd> and <kbd>Ctrl/Cmd+Shift+Z</kbd> to redo.
-   **Row/Column Actions:** Right-click a row or column header (or any cell) to insert or delete. The number of rows/columns inserted is based on your selection size.
-   **Data Analysis**: An integrated statistics modal for data distribution and correlation analysis.
-   **Theming**: Includes light and dark mode support, with an easy-to-use theme object.
